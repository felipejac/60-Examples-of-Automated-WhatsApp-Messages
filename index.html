Chapter 3: The Ritual: 6 Tips for Successful Implementation

Integrating automated WhatsApp messages to interact with customers is a powerful strategy for customer service, marketing, and sales. However, to ensure these messages are well-received and contribute to a positive experience, it is essential to adopt specific practices.

That's why we're presenting 6 fundamental tips for implementing automated messages for customers effectively.

3.1 Know Your Audience

Before you implement any of these ghostly messages, you need to understand the preferences, behaviors, and interaction patterns of your audience. This is critical for giving you a good idea of what kind of message you should send.

Segment your messages based on demographics, purchase history, or specific interests. You can send an abandoned cart message via WhatsApp, as well as sales messages and thank you messages.

For example, if your company offers diverse products, segment messages related to specific categories of interest for each customer group.

Learn more about the different types of segmentation: demographic segmentation, psychographic segmentation, and behavioral segmentation.

3.2 Personalize Your Communications

Use personalized fields to insert not only the customer's name but also information related to their past interactions.

For example, if a customer bought a specific product, your automated messages can include recommendations for complementary products, creating a personalized and relevant experience.

Test for free (for 14 days!) how ActiveCampaign's personalized fields work! Learn more about how to send personalized automated messages on WhatsApp. Don't miss this opportunity and get more customers.

3.3 Be Transparent and Offer Control

At the beginning of the interaction, clearly state that you're using automated messages and provide customization options.

For example, allow customers to choose the frequency of messages or offer a simple command, such as "stop," to disable automated messages at any time. This transparency helps build a relationship of trust with customers.

3.4 Offer Real Value

Make sure your automated WhatsApp messages deliver something of value to customers. For example, if you send promotional messages, include exclusive discounts or early access to sales.

If the messages aim to provide information, make sure they are relevant and useful to the recipients. Real value in messages encourages participation and customer loyalty.

3.5 Respect the Time

Schedule your messages to be sent considering time zones and the most opportune times for interaction.

In that sense, avoid sending promotional messages too early in the morning or late at night. Also, consider the post-purchase moment to send thank you messages and request feedback, demonstrating care for the customer experience.

3.6 Create Interactive Messages

Include calls to action (CTAs) in messages to encourage interaction. So, in a post-purchase follow-up message, invite customers to share their experiences or participate in satisfaction surveys.

These interactions not only increase engagement but also provide valuable insights into customer preferences.
